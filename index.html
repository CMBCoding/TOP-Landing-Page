Welcome to Obsidian Biomedical Technologies

The Cutting Edge

A pioneer in the ever expanding field of biomedicine; Obsidian Biomedical Technologies is committed to leaving no stone unturned, no avenue unexplored in our endless efforts to build a better humanity

Pioneering New Frontiers

Cybernetics

Erasing the boundaries between man and machine, Obsidian Cybernetics is our newest Division, working to find ways to synergize the natural with the artificial; creating responsive prosthetics to help those dealt a cruel hand become whole again.

Genetic Research

Nature is often cruel. Countless maladies stemming from faults on the genetic level cause undue suffering to millions worldwide. From sunny San Diego, the Obsidian Genetic Research Facility gathers the best and brightest from around the world to cure humanity of its deficits.

Engineering

Obsidian does not believe in waiting for new breakthroughs to emerge. Instead, we take active steps in advancing medical technology. Our New Jersey-based Applied Engineering Division works to develop new machines and medical devices; ensuring we remain on the forefront of new developments.

Biological Research

There is an endless wealth of knowledge to be gained from studying the natural world. The researchers and caretakers at the Obsidian Biological Research Preserve endeavor to unravel the mysteries behind the miraculous abilities found in the animal kingdom.
